## 4. FAQ（可直接上商品页）

### What does the necklace do?
**It contains an NFC chip that opens the Guardian Eye experience on your phone. From there, you select what feels true and receive a short sign back.**

### Do I need an app?
**No app needed. Just tap with a compatible phone to open the experience.**

### Will it work on every phone?
**It works on most modern smartphones with NFC enabled. Exact tap sensitivity can vary by device and phone case.**

### Is this for daily use?
**Yes. You can wear it like a regular necklace and tap in whenever you want a small emotional reset.**

### Is it fortune telling?
**No. It’s designed as a reflective ritual experience, not a prediction tool.**

### Can I save or share my result?
**Yes. The result screen is designed to be screenshot-friendly and easy to share.**

### Is the necklace waterproof?
**Treat it like fashion jewelry: avoid prolonged water exposure, sweat buildup, and chemicals to help preserve the finish.**
